Pastor Albert

On 7th October 1976 a boy was born as a youngest son of among three children for James Loppas and Thresya Loppas; in a remote village of Thiruvananthapuram in the Kerala. He was born and bought up in a poor family, and by his birth his mother accepted Jesus as her personal savior and now whole family accepted Jesus.

While he was 17 years old he accepted Jesus as his personal savior on 28th November 1992 and on 7th March 1993 received baptism from Assembly of God Church Veliyannoor by PR.P.S.PHILIP superintend Malayalam district council.

On 3rd June 1993 began his bible study in different Bible Schools, Seminary and Colleges for Nine years along with doing HIS ministry. By the grace of God on 1999 he came to North India as a missionary and still shines for the glory of the Lord Jesus Christ. On 2003 he formed L4LM with prayer and fasting.It was a grace of God to do HIS ministry in 23 Indian states as a evangelist, pastor and teacher; at present ministering in Chandigarh.

Albert T.L James married with Sajitha Albert D/O. Yesudasan& Sumathy from Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala and have on daughter named Aldina S. Albert studying in LKG.
